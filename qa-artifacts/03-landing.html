  • Learner profile & declaration
  • Attendance & participation
  • Communication, email & telephone evidence

  • Customer service, technology & business documents

  • You submit POE items online

Frequently asked questions

Is this the official QCTO qualification?

The Occupational Certificate: Office Administrator is a QCTO qualification. Until the official curriculum document, learner guide, workbook, assessment guide and POE guide are uploaded by the provider, the platform runs in demo content mode — clearly labelled — and is structured to accept the official documents as soon as they are available.

How is the POE submitted?

All POE items are completed online. You can save drafts, upload evidence files, and submit for review. The platform generates a final PDF POE pack with full audit trail.

Who assesses my work?

Your facilitator gives feedback. Your assessor makes the formal competence decision. A sample is sent for internal moderation. Workplace evidence is signed off by your supervisor.

Can I learn offline?

Yes. The platform is PWA-ready. Drafts of your workbook and POE are saved locally and synced when you're back online.

What if my payment is pending?

You can still log in, but learning and POE access is locked until your payment is confirmed. The rest of the portal is still browsable.

Is my data safe?

The platform is built with POPIA-aware design: role-based access control, private file storage, audit trail, and consent placeholders.
